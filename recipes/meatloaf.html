Meafloaf

Description

When you're in need of delicious comfort food that everyone in your household will love, a traditional meatloaf is the way to go. It's a classic meal, and it's an incredibly simple one too.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chuck
  • Vegetables (carrot, celery, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and garlic)
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Egg
  • Spices (dried Italian herbs, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper)
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Glaze (brown sugar, ketchup, Dijon mustard, and sriracha)

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables.
  2. Combine the vegetables, ground chuck, Worcestershire sauce, spices, and egg.
  3. Form the meat mixture into a loaf and place it in a prepared baking pan.
  4. Bake for about 15 minutes.
  5. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, Dijon, and sriracha to make the glaze.
  6. Spoon the glaze over the slightly hot meatloaf.
  7. Return meatloaf to the oven and bake until the internal temperature reaches 160 degrees F.
