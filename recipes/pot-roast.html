Pot Roast

Description

A quick and easy recipe for pot roasts. This recipe is perfect for beginner chefs or those without much time on their hands to prepare dinner.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 ½ cups baby carrots
  • 1 (3 pound) beef roast
  • 3 cubes beef bouillon
  • ½ cup water

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Cut potatoes, onions, and celery into fairly large chunks.
  3. Place in the bottom of a slow cooker along with baby carrots.
  4. Place roast on top of vegetables.
  5. Place 3 bouillon cubes randomly on top of the roast and pour in 1/2 cup water.
  6. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours or on High for 4 to 5 hours.
  7. Transfer roast to a platter.
  8. Remove vegetables using a slotted spoon and arrange them around the roast.
