Seafood Boil

Description

The dish is a seasoned medley of boiled corn, shrimp, sausage, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning (such as Old Bay), or to taste
  • 5 pounds new potatoes
  • 3 (16 ounce) packages cooked kielbasa sausage, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 8 ears fresh corn, husks and silks removed
  • 5 pounds whole crab, broken into pieces
  • 4 pounds fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat a large pot of water over an outdoor cooker.
  3. Add seafood seasoning to taste and bring to a boil.
  4. Add potatoes and sausage and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Break corn ears in half; add corn and crab and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add shrimp when potatoes are almost tender and everything else is almost done.
  7. Cook until shrimp are just cooked through, 3 to 4 minutes longer.
  8. Drain off and discard the water.
